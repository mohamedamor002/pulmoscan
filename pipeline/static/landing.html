Our Technology

Built on cutting-edge deep learning and medical imaging technology

Advanced MRI Analysis

Our advanced MRI analysis technology allows for real-time visualization and processing of medical images. The AI-powered system can detect anomalies and provide insights that might be missed by the human eye.

Using state-of-the-art computer vision algorithms, PulmoScan processes thousands of image slices to create a comprehensive 3D model of the patient's lungs, enabling more accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

Deep Learning Architecture

PulmoScan uses a specialized convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture trained on over 100,000 annotated CT slices. Our models are continuously improved through regular retraining with new validated data, ensuring the highest detection rates while minimizing false positives.

  • Dual-pathway 3D CNN for detection and classification
  • 98% detection accuracy and 95% classification accuracy
  • Validated on diverse international datasets
  • Regular performance monitoring and updates

About PulmoScan

Dedicated to revolutionizing lung cancer detection

PulmoScan was founded by a team of radiologists, AI researchers, and healthcare innovators with a shared mission: to improve early detection of lung cancer and save lives.
